Our Facilities

At Shwetambari Public School, we provide world-class facilities to ensure that every student enjoys a balanced environment of academics, sports, creativity, and overall development.

Smart Classrooms

Equipped with interactive boards and digital content, our smart classrooms make learning engaging and effective.

Library

Our library houses thousands of books, journals, and digital resources to encourage reading and research habits in students.

Science Labs

Fully equipped Physics, Chemistry, and Biology labs provide hands-on learning experiences for curious young minds.

Computer Lab

A modern computer lab with high-speed internet fosters technological skills and creativity among students.

Sports Grounds

Spacious playgrounds and courts encourage physical fitness, teamwork, and excellence in various sports.

School Transport

Safe and reliable school buses with GPS tracking ensure convenient travel for students across the city.

Hostel

Our well-furnished hostel provides a homely atmosphere with nutritious food and round-the-clock care for boarders.

Art & Music Rooms

Creative spaces dedicated to art, music, and dance help students express their talents and passions freely.
